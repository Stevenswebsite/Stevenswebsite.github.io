6th Grade

In 6th grade we learned the basics of computer programs like typing, microsoft excel, word, Java script, and power point.

We did projects with all of these like Elephants on word, Coding art with Java script, and classroom favorites. In this grade by the end you needed to type 30wpm.

7th Grade

We learned more about pictures and art.

We used programs like adobe illustrator, photoshop, and sketchup.

We made projects on these like the icecream, my house, and magazine cover. At the end of the year we had 45wpm.

8th Grade

Finally, the last year. We learned coding, garageband, filming, storyboard, and Imovie.

These programs have showed me what goes on in the background of movies. We had to make a short film for a contest. I had done my on spellebrity. We had to plan it out on storyboard then film it the use garage band for voice and Imovie to put it all together. Also by the end of the year we had to type 60wpm.
